Welcome to DeliBox

About

DeliBox is designed to receive use parcels automatically and securely without the users physical presence. It can be operated via telegram and the unique otp generation feature with each delivery ensures the security of your parcels. A weight sensor and an alarm system along with a sonar are incorporated to further increase our products reliability.
